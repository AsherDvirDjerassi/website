About

Asher Dvir-Djerassi is a joint Ph.D. candidate in Sociology and Public Policy. He is a Fellow at the Stone Center for Inequality Dynamics, where he works closely with the Wealth and Mobility (WAM) Study. As a Population Center Trainee, he has been funded by the National Institutes of Health.
